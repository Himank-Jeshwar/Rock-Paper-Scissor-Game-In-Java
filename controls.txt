Main Menu Controls 
Main Menu has buttons for :-
	Press 'P' to Play
	Press 'I' to Read Instructions
	Press 'E' to Exit
The Game Controls are :-
	Press 'R' for Rock
	Press 'S' for Scissor
	Press 'P' for Paper

The game has very simple user interface and controls.
